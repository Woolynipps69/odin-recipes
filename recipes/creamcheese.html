Cream Cheese Icing

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces room temp cream cheese
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 4 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup powdered sugar

Directions

In a large bowl mix all the Ingredients listed above and enjoy.

This goes great with any type of cake or our cinnamon buns.
